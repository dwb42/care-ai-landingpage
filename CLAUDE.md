# CLAUDE.md

## Purpose

This repo contains the landing page / website for **Caro**.

Caro helps people in Germany with topics like:
- Pflegegrad
- Pflegegeld
- Pflegeleistungen
- Begutachtung
- Widerspruch

Your job is to build a landing page that feels **calm, trustworthy, clear, human, and competent**.

This must **not** feel like a generic SaaS page or flashy AI startup.

---

## Main goal

The page has one primary goal:

> Make the user feel enough trust and clarity to start the chat with Caro.

Primary CTA:
- `Chat starten`
- `Jetzt kostenlos starten`

---

## Audience

Users are often:
- emotionally stressed
- unfamiliar with the process
- afraid of mistakes
- overwhelmed by bureaucracy
- looking for simple, clear guidance

Design and copy must reduce stress and create orientation.

---

## Tone

Write in German.

Tone must be:
- clear
- calm
- respectful
- human
- trustworthy
- non-salesy

Avoid:
- hype
- AI buzzwords
- startup language
- exaggerated promises
- manipulative urgency

Do not use words like:
- revolutionary
- disruptive
- cutting-edge
- game changer
- next-generation

Prefer phrases like:
- verständlich erklärt
- Schritt für Schritt begleitet
- ohne Formulardschungel
- einfach per Chat
- für Angehörige und Betroffene

---

## Design principles

The page should feel:
- modern
- light
- high quality
- calm
- warm
- easy to scan

Use:
- lots of whitespace
- strong typography hierarchy
- soft rounded corners
- subtle shadows
- restrained colors
- mobile-first layout

Avoid:
- loud gradients
- neon colors
- visual clutter
- cliché startup visuals
- stock-photo energy
- overly technical design

Optimize for trust, not excitement.

---

## UX principles

Above the fold, make clear within seconds:
1. what Caro helps with
2. who it is for
3. why it feels easier
4. what the next step is

Use a simple structure:
1. Hero
2. Für wen ist das?
3. Was Caro hilft
4. So funktioniert es
5. Warum das anders ist
6. FAQ
7. Final CTA
8. Footer

Keep the page:
- easy to scan
- low friction
- low cognitive load
- mobile-friendly

---

## Trust rules

Build trust through:
- clear language
- calm design
- honest framing
- visible friction reducers like:
  - Keine Anmeldung
  - Auch per Sprachnachricht
  - Schritt für Schritt begleitet

Do not use:
- fake testimonials
- fake ratings
- fake trust badges
- legal or medical guarantees
- claims that Caro replaces official institutions

Caro may guide, explain, prepare, and support.
Do not imply guaranteed outcomes.

---

## Technical rules

- Inspect the existing codebase first
- Use the existing stack and patterns
- Avoid unnecessary dependencies
- Keep components reusable
- Keep code simple and maintainable
- Make text easy to edit
- Prioritize mobile quality
- Use semantic HTML and accessible structure

---

## Workflow

For non-trivial work:
1. inspect existing code
2. make a short plan
3. implement
4. review mobile layout
5. review tone, trust, and clarity
6. summarize changes

---

## Definition of done

The page is done when:
- it feels calm and credible
- the hero is clear
- the CTA is obvious
- the layout works well on mobile
- the copy is understandable
- the page does not feel like generic startup marketing
- trust is built through clarity, not gimmicks

---

## Final reminder

When in doubt, choose:
- trust over hype
- clarity over cleverness
- calm over intensity
- usefulness over feature density
